# Corrections And Challenges

This is the permanent correction channel for *The Token Is Not the Financial
Instrument* and the bounded Tokenized Financial Instrument Integrity public
evidence package.

Submit a correction or challenge to `rampaidarwin@gmail.com` with the subject
`TFII 001 correction`.

Include:

1. the paper section, claim ID, record ID, or artifact path;
2. the exact statement challenged;
3. the proposed correction;
4. an authoritative source and precise locator; and
5. whether the issue concerns fact, scope, calculation, rights, or subsequent
   change.

Receipt is not acceptance. Each submission is classified as supported,
partially supported, unsupported, superseded, or unresolved. Accepted changes
receive a correction identifier, effective date, prior and replacement hashes,
and propagation record. Released versions are never silently overwritten.

The absence of a response from a named subject is not confirmation. A later
supported correction may change a field or conclusion without retroactively
turning the earlier bounded record into misconduct or misrepresentation.
